Gallery Neumorphism

This gallery showcases the neumorphism design trend, which combines background colors, shadows, and highlights to create a soft, 3D effect. If you look closely, you can see how the elements seem to float above the background.

If you click on the images, a modal will open with a larger view of the image and additional information.
